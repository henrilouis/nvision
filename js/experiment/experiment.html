This study is joint project of the ‘Big Data’ department of NSPYRE and the ‘Human-Technology Interaction’ department of the Eindhoven University of Technology, conducted by Henri Schröter and supervised by dr.ir. Martijn Willemsen.

In this study into the usability of information visualization systems you will be asked to complete a few assignments in your browser using a system called NVISION, followed by a short questionnaire. The study will take approximately fifteen minutes.

All research conducted at the Human-Technology Interaction Group adheres to the Code of Ethics of the NIP (Nederlands Instituut voor Psychologen – Dutch Institute for Psychologists). We will not be sharing personal information about you to anyone outside of the research team. The information that we collect is used for writing scientific publications and will be reported at group level. It will be completely anonymous and it cannot be traced back to you.

Assignment introduction

You work at a government organisation analyzing cars. Your manager Bob wants you to create him a dashboard with three visualization-sheets he can inspect. He provides you with a dataset containing information about cars in the US between 1970 and 1982. Bob has four assignments for you to complete. Try to solve the assignments as efficiently as possible.

Be sure to make the first two assingment individual sheets.

Assignment 1

I need a comparison of powerful but economical cars. Create a scatter chart on the first sheet for me. Then plot power and economy on it. For this comparison I only want to see the cars with power of 160 horsepower and above. How much horsepower does the most economical car in the visualization have?

When your answer is correct the continue button will become green.

Incorrect, please try again. Hint: a higher number in economy (mpg) means the car is more economical.

Assignment 2

Next I require you to create a new sheet containing the bar chart visualization. It should display the names of the cars and their acceleration ( 0-60 mph ). A requirement is that they are built in 1980 and later. Please write down the name of the fastest car.

Incorrect, please try again. Hints: a smaller number in acceleration means the car is faster.

Assignment 3

Good, we have all the required sheets. Make sure you get familiar with the filtering process.

Now filter so only cars with five to seven cylinders and economy (mpg) of 15 and more are included in the previously created sheets. What is the brand of the slowest accelerating ( 0-60 mph ) car on the second sheet?

Incorrect, please try again.

Assignment 4

With the previously assigned filtering in place, reinspect the fist sheet with the visualization of power and economy. What is the name of the only car left on this sheet?

Incorrect, please try again.
